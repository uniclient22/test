Business Visa Requirement

  • 01
    Relevant Pages of Passport: Nepali national passport with validity of at least 3 months after the end of the planned journey. Copy of the identity page of your passport and copy of the most recent Schengen visa with stamps.
  • 02
    Visa Application Form: The visa application must be filled with proper information and signed by the applicant. Please find the visa application form below
  • 03
    Photograph: 1 recent color photograph (5x3cm) with white background glued; taken within the last 6 months.
  • 04
    Photo ID card/ Business Card: A personal identity card of the applicant such as National ID/Adhar Card/Driving License and business card (Recommended).
  • 05
    Cover Letter: A personal letter from applicant including personal details, purpose of visit and outlining who will be responsible for the full costs of the trip. The letter should be addressed to – The Visa Officer, The respective visa office/embassy and signed by the applicant. Visa Category must be mentioned in the subject line.
  • 06
    Invitation Letter from a registered company/organization: Original/scanned copy invitation letter from the Invitee organization on its letter-head pad stating the purpose of travel, duration of stay and other necessary details with invitee's signature.
  • 07
    Invitee's photo ID Card Copy: A clear scanned copy of photo ID of the invitee/host in the visiting country. (Preferably attested by a legal authenticator).
  • 08
    Forwarding Letter from employer: Confirmation letter of the company in Nepal explaining the purpose and duration of the journey and the guarantee to cover all costs that may arise during the stay in Belgium.
  • 09
    NOC/LOI: A letter from your employer on the organization's letter-head pad, with contact details, stating that a leave of absence has been granted, purpose and duration of the trip, and that you will be returning to your current job as per schedule.
  • 10
    Personal Bank Statements: Original Bank Statements (Personal/Payroll) for last 6 months mentioning the Bank’s name, telephone number clearly with authorized bank stamp and signature (e-statement is not acceptable).
  • 11
    Sponsor's Bank Statements: Original/scanned copy of bank statements from the sponsoring party (invitee/Employer Company/any other person) for last 6 months mentioning the Bank’s name, telephone number clearly with authorized bank stamp and signature.
  • 12
    Travel Health Insurance: travel health insurance/medical insurance covering the full time of an applicant's intended visit. The insurance must be valid throughout the territory of the visiting state/s. The insurance must be of minimum 30000/- coverage.
  • 13
    Airline Ticket Itinerary: A reserved itinerary for your airline ticket as per the schedule of the trip.
  • 14
    Hotel Reservation: A reserved itinerary for your accommodation at a hotel or resort as per the schedule of the trip.
