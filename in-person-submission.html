In-person Submission

Nepalese citizens get their visas stamped in-person from designated consular offices for 56 countries

In person submission means the applicant need to submit his/her visa application by his own to the embassy/consulate office or Visa Application Center. Some countries embassy may require an interview of the applicant or biometric fingerprint submission to process their application so that is why in-person submission is required from applicants end. In this cases, applicants may have to take appointment from the embassy before the submission through online.
